    About Us

    Mindxet Nigeria is a social enterprise with a mission to develop young people, entrepreneurs, local small and medium businesses in Nigeria through mentorship, media campaign, public speaking, career counselling, youth mentorship, unmatched business strategy, talent management and more. We have over the years provided mentorship and support for small and indigenous businesses. We are based in Port Harcourt but our services are available online and offline across Nigeria.

    Services

    We provide youths and SMEs with the necessary skills and knowledge to be successful in their chosen path ranging from entrepreneurship to business growth, career and personal development, soft skills development etc. At Mindxet Nigeria, we are passionate, creative, hardworking and committed to seeing people and businesses succeed, and we are here to stay!

    Youth Mentoring

    Our youth mentoring program matches volunteer mentors with youths across Nigeria to empower young people to become a positive in their community.

    Media Campaign

    We propel our clients' marketing performance with radio and television campaigns that build brand engagement and support the customer journey.

    Public Speaking Training

    Be a better speaker - with the right public speaking skills, you can build speeches and presentations, and deliver them in a way that makes an impact!

    Voice-over

    We offer professional voice-over services (audio, video, elearning) at highly affordable rates. Contact us for samples; any Nigerian language, any voice, any tone and any industry.

    HR Consulting

    An organization's sustained triumph is reliant on the quality of its people. We collaborate with selected strategic partners nationwide to ensure services are relevant to your locale focusing on helping local SMEs attract, win, incorporate and retain quality talents.

    Career Counselling

    For those undecided about their careers or interested in learning more about career development and decision making, we offer assessments to match interests and personality with appropriate careers, and courses in career planning.
